The Program

Coaching built around the jump

A complete development approach for young triple & long jumpers — from the approach run to the landing pit. Here's what we work on.

◇

Technique

Approach, takeoff, and in-air mechanics broken down step by step and rebuilt for consistency and distance.

➔

Approach & Rhythm

⚡

Speed Development

Bounding, hops, and explosive strength to turn speed into elastic, powerful jumps.
